## About the Events Section

INC (Intermedia Network Center) plans to host a variety of performances and events in VRChat on an ongoing basis.


This section will list upcoming events as they are confirmed.

## Types of Events

INC plans to host the following kinds of events:

- Music performances and concerts
- Academic and technology talks
- Exhibition and gallery events
- Meetups and social gatherings
- Other events related to culture and the arts

## How to Participate

You can join the event via a VRChat world or group. Please check the individual event pages for details.

## Updates

Event information will be updated here on an ongoing basis. Be sure to check the INC group announcements for the latest news.
