## Entrance

When you join the world, you will spawn in the Entrance. There is a quiz here; after answering it correctly, enter the elevator to warp to the Main Hall.

![Entrance](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 003612.png)

## Main Hall

The interior features an open layout where all areas are connected. The following facilities are located within the Main Hall.

### Café Area

Located to the left of the warp arrival point in the Main Hall. It features a coffee-making gadget and snacks.

![Café Area](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 003751.png)

### Work Area

This area features a figure-eight-shaped desk. The following equipment is available:

- **VizVid**: A video player. Please use it for live streaming your work or watching videos. If the nearby sign says "In Use," the display is being used by another user, so please use a different one.
- **ImagePad**: You can display images by entering a URL.

![Work Area](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 004039.png)

### Lounge Area

This space features several sofas.

![Lounge Area](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 004158.png)

### Quiet Zone

There are two areas with sheer curtains that block out external noise. These spaces are designed for those who wish to work or converse in a quiet environment.

![Quiet Zone](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 004232.png)

### Piano

A piano is available for anyone to play.

![Piano](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 004305.png)

### Group Posters

Posters featuring groups introduced by INC are displayed here. There are four near the café area and four on the left wall next to the piano.

![Group Posters](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 004430.png)

### Gallery Area

Descend the spiral staircase to the basement to find the gallery area. Art exhibitions are held here during events.

## DJ Area

Located at the far end of the main hall. In addition to playing pre-set playlists, DJ sets are permitted. Permissions for DJ sets are scheduled to be expanded gradually.

## Garden Area

A circular outdoor area extending above the main hall. This spacious area can be used as a place for conversation and socializing among large groups.
