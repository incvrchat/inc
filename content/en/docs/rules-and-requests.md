# INC Etiquette
## About Quiet Rooms

Quiet rooms are areas designed for those who wish to concentrate on work or conversation. Please refrain from staying in these rooms for extended periods while not actively using them, such as when AFK.

## About the Workspace

The workspace is equipped with ImagePad and VizVid. You are free to use these tools, but please refrain from monopolizing them or using them in ways that interfere with other users.

## About Promotional Activities

Please refrain from prolonged promotional activities for groups, services, etc., using avatars or other means.
