# Introduction to Menu Features
You can customize your settings within INC by using the menu.

## How to Open the Menu

[Image]

You can open the menu in each environment using the following methods.

For VR: Press the right trigger on the Quest controller twice to display the menu.

For Desktop: Hold down the Tab key to display the menu.

## Explanation of Menu Items

### Feature Toggle

[Image]

In "Feature Toggle," you can configure settings such as world optimization and colliders.

Gimmick On/Off: You can show or hide gimmicks within the world.

Light & Effects On/Off: You can enable or disable avatar lights and post-effects within the world.

Colliders On/Off: You can enable or disable colliders for objects such as chairs within the world.

### Map

[Image]

In "Map," you can check your own and other users' locations and teleport within the world.

The red icon on the map indicates your location.

The black icons indicate the locations of other users.

You can teleport to various areas within the world using the buttons on the left.

### QvPen Call

[Image]

In "QvPen Call," you can summon the QvPen or eraser.

To summon the pen, you can either select an unused pen or choose one from the palette on the right.

### Sound Settings

[Image]

In "Sound Settings," you can turn the world's BGM and ambient sounds on or off.

### System Settings

[Image]

In "System Settings," you can change the language and adjust the menu size.

### Version Information

"Version Information" displays the version of the gimmick and credits for Yukineko, the menu creator.

INC's menu functionality is provided based on Yukineko's "World Integrated Menu."
