# For Those Interested in Posting Posters
INC is currently accepting submissions for posters to be displayed within our virtual world.

![Group Poster Display Area](/inc/assets/images/content/docs/スクリーンショット 2026-04-29 004430.png)

Prior to the official start of the call for submissions, we would like to share INC’s philosophy regarding poster placement and an overview of the submission process.

## INC’s Vision for Advertising
In this virtual world, we are exploring new ways for people and advertising to interact within a VR environment.

Rather than advertisements that simply convey information one-sidedly or are avoided as noise, we aim to display posters that naturally make visitors to INC stop in their tracks and take a positive interest.

We hope you will utilize the poster space as a medium to enrich the spatial experience and connect those who are active in the world with its visitors.
## Overview of the Poster Call for Submissions
### Display Fees
We plan to offer this service free of charge.
## Eligible Applicants
**Individuals and Groups**: Those engaged in community activities (such as group management or event organization) on platforms like VRChat. Limited to non-profit purposes.

**Corporations**: We will need to discuss this on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us.

## Design Philosophy
At INC, based on the philosophy outlined above, we place great importance on the design quality of posters. 

We plan to review submissions based on whether the design harmonizes with the World’s space and effectively conveys its appeal to visitors.
## Upcoming Schedule
We plan to open the next poster submission period in early June 2026.

More detailed submission guidelines, including poster specifications and submission methods, will be announced on our website and the organizers’ X account around that time.

If you’d like to promote your community or activities through the INC space, please look forward to the detailed announcement in June.
