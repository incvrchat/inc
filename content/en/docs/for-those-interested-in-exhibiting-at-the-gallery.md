# About the Gallery
The gallery area is designed as a space located at the bottom of a spiral staircase. Up to 10 works can be displayed comfortably on the walls. It provides a serene space where visitors can immerse themselves in the artworks.
## Call for Submissions
The INC Gallery welcomes a diverse range of artistic expressions that go beyond two-dimensional illustrations and photographs.

**Wide Range of Supported Media:** In addition to images, we can exhibit works such as 3D models and pieces utilizing shaders, regardless of genre or artistic technique.

**High-Definition Image Display:** For image-based works, we support display at up to 4K resolution. This allows you to convey your artistic intent to visitors in full detail, down to the finest brushstrokes and textural nuances.

**Display of Work Information:** Each exhibition space allows for the display of the work’s title and a caption. You are free to include details such as the work’s concept or technical background.

## We Are Currently Accepting Submissions

INC is always accepting submissions of artwork to showcase in our gallery area. Please take advantage of this opportunity to share your work with the many users who visit our venue.

Detailed regulations regarding submissions and the latest recruitment status will be announced on this webpage and via the organizer’s X account.
